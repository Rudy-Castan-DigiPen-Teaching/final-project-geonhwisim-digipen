You can move your character by w, a, s, d.

If you're in down wall side you can do only going to rightside(d key) or jumping(w key).
Also if you're in right wall side you can do only going to upperside(w key) or jumping(a key).
Upper wall side you can do only going to leftside(a key) or jumping(s key).
Right wall side you can do only going to downside(s key) or jumping(d key).

And you can change the wall side by pressing space bar when you get close to the wall some extent.

Collect the googly eyes you'll win.
And if you collide to bouncing ball and wall you'll lose.
